How much does a WebQuix project cost?
+
Our engagements typically start at ₹35,000 for focused projects and scale based on scope. We don't offer ₹5,000 websites — because they don't generate ROI. Every proposal is custom-scoped after understanding your specific goals and business model.
How long does a typical project take?
+
Standard website and landing page engagements: 3–6 weeks. Full-stack applications and custom platforms: 8–16 weeks. We move quickly without sacrificing quality — our process eliminates the revision cycles that waste time on both sides.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
+
We don't start building until you've approved the strategy and design direction. Our staged approval process means you're never surprised by a final deliverable. If something isn't right, we revise it — as many times as needed within scope.
Do you work with small businesses?
+
Absolutely. We work with startup founders, small and medium businesses, coaches, consultants, and e-commerce brands across India. The right fit isn't about company size — it's about your mindset toward ROI and quality.
Will I own and be able to manage my website?
+
Yes, completely. Every project is handed over with full documentation, training, and admin access. Whether built on WordPress, Webflow, Shopify or Next.js — you own it and can manage it independently. We also offer maintenance retainers for ongoing support.
Do you offer support after launch?
+
Yes. Our Maintenance & Support retainers include proactive monitoring, security updates, performance reporting, content updates, and a dedicated point of contact. Plans start at ₹5,000/month — structured so you're always paying for ongoing value.
